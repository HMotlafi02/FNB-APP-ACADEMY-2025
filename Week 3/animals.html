ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and long trunks.

GEMSBOK

The Gemsbok is a large antelope known for its striking appearance, with long, straight horns and a distinctive black and white facial pattern.

GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and legs. They are native to Africa and primarily feed on leaves from tall trees.

GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest primates and are known for their strength and intelligence. They live in groups called troops and are primarily found in the forests of Africa.

KOALA

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their tree-dwelling lifestyle and diet of eucalyptus leaves. They are often called "koala bears," but they are not bears.

LION

Lions are large carnivorous mammals known as the "king of the jungle." They live in prides and are native to Africa and parts of Asia.

MONKEY

Monkeys are primates known for their agility and intelligence. They are found in various habitats around the world and are known for their social behavior.

PANDA

Pandas are large bears native to China, known for their distinctive black and white fur. They primarily eat bamboo and are considered a symbol of conservation efforts.

WARTHOGS

Warthogs are wild pigs native to Africa, known for their distinctive tusks and wart-like growths on their faces. They are often found in savannas and grasslands.
